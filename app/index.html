What is Study Pack?
Study Pack is a mobile education platform designed for senior secondary school students


Contents are based on the Nigerian Curriculum and syllabus. The platform is available to schools and students in participating states and the FCT. We use technology to elavate the quality of teaching and learning. First by making learning more interactive for today's generation of students. Secondly by bridging the gap between students in remote / rural areas and talented professional teachers accross Nigeria.

Technology is changing the world and the way we live

Like having over 12,000 past questions (with complete answers and solutions) for JAMB, WAEC, and NECO, all stored on your smartphone or mobile device.

Like learning directly from some of the best secondary school teachers in Nigeria, through video lessons stored on your phone or mobile device.

All these and more are now possible and it works even when you don't have internet connection. This is education for the new generation.

Get The Right Teachers

We know people learn differently, which is why we keep adding new video lessons taught by different professional teachers, with diverse teaching methods. Video and lessons are between 5 to 10 minutes. They cover the entire syllabus of the three major exams in Nigerian secondary schools. No matter your academic situation, we have a teacher for you.

Practise Makes Perfect

A major key to success in exams is completing as many practise questions as possible. Your Study Pack has over 10,000 real past questions (with answers and detailed solutions). It has tools to help you with time and speed for the exam. No paid subscription required. And all contents can be downloaded on your phone, so no need to spend on data or internet connection.

You Will Remember More

A picture says more than a thousand words and students remember more when engaged in audio visual formats. You don't only get to read and solve past questions, you can also watch video lessons. This makes it easier to understand the topics and remember the fundamental concepts. You don't have to forget anything, it is all on your phone.

Watch And Learn From Good Teachers

You now have access to over 180 video lessons, covering topics taught in most subjects at the senior secondary school level. The teachers are very engaging and sometimes very funny. They use examples that connect with everyday nigerian life and society. Our library of video lessons are continously updated, with new additions coming from newly discovered teachers and experts. No matter where you live or school now you can watch and learn from one of the best teachers available. It is all on your Phone / Tablet.

Learn With Any Device You Like

Whether you use a smartphone, tablet, laptop or desktop computer, you can access your Study Pack when you want to. It does not matter if you device runs on Android or iOS, you can download Study Pack on both platforms. And the best part is after the initial download, you no longer need internet connection to use the app, it is all saved on your phone. This way you save money on your data plan.

No Guessing, Learn To Tell The Right Option

Sometimes you cannot know all the answers and sometimes it looks like one answer is correct. You Study Pack teaches you ways to identify the right options and you will also learn how to focus on the right topics and concepts. Save your time and stress less by studying mainly those topics that will be tested in the exam.

As Exams Evolve, Students Have To Evolve

Examinations are evolving in the way they are administered, some like the UTME have migrated from the pencil and paper format to the computer based format (CBT).Also the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board has adopted the item response approach in setting questions in the UTME. These are major game changers and wise students need to study with materials that match these new changes. The Study Pack platform has been designed to deliver such advantages to students.
